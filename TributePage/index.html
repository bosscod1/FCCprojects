Who is Elon Musk?

Elon Musk is a billionaire entrepreneur, engineer, and inventor known for revolutionizing multiple industries. He is the CEO of **Tesla**, **SpaceX**, **Neuralink**, and **The Boring Company**.

His mission is to **make life multi-planetary** by colonizing Mars with **SpaceX**, revolutionize transport with **Tesla**, and push AI-human interaction with **Neuralink**.

Musk has also contributed to **renewable energy**, **autonomous driving**, and **Hyperloop transport systems**.
